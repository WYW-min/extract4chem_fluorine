17: # Introduction
18: 
19: Progress in modern technology dictates the necessity of the new composite polymer materials for creation with wide range of specific physical properties. One of the new direction [1] of material investigation is the development of the special polymer mixing technology in order to design the new constructs with high operating performances. Due to the high thermal and radiation stability of polyimides (PIs), there are endless possibilities of obtaining blends based on this class of materials [1-3]. However the formation of composites on the basis of polyimides is not simple task. It is determined by low solubility and double-staged process as well as strong exchange processes of equilibrium system di-anhydride  $^+$  diamine  $=$  polyamidoacid [4-5].
