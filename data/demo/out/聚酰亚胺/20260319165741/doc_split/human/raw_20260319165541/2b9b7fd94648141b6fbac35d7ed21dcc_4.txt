55: The IR-spectra of PETP shows characteristic absorption bands in the region of  $1735~\mathrm{cm}^{-1}$ ,  $1110~\mathrm{cm}^{-1}$  and  $1220~\mathrm{cm}^{-1}$ ,  $830~\mathrm{cm}^{-1}$  that are related to valency oscillations of the bonds  $\nu (\mathrm{C = O})$  at  $1735~\mathrm{cm}^{-1}$  and  $\nu (\mathrm{C - O - C})$  at 1110 and  $1220~\mathrm{cm}^{-1}$  respectively and to deformation bonds oscillations (C-H) at  $830~\mathrm{cm}^{-1}$ . There are also bands at  $1110~\mathrm{cm}^{-1}$  due to ester (C-O-C), at  $2960~\mathrm{cm}^{-1} - (\mathrm{C - H})$  and also at  $1500~\mathrm{cm}^{-1}$  that is related to valency oscillations of the  $-\mathrm{C = C - }$  benzene ring [6].

79: The morphology of the film surfaces, which were formed by mechanical and reaction mixing were investigated by optical microscopy that helps to judge the sizes and thin structure of domains. The general picture of blend morphology represents transparent homogenous polyimide matrix with uniformly distributed particles of disperse phase PETP at its content less than  $15\mathrm{wt.\%}$  (Fig. 5). The Figure 5 shows that under reaction mixing it is possible to obtain deeper miscibility that results in formation of homogenous and fine-disperse structure. One of the main reasons is condensation of upgrowing polyimide chains along PETP matrix during chemical way of blending. It could cause fine mixing of two polymers at molecular level. Higher content of PETP (above  $15\mathrm{wt.\%}$ ) promotes the beginning of phase separation which manifests in opaque morphology of blends (data does not show).

90: The addition of P2 into the initial PI causes increasing content of non-cyclized amide groups of PI from 9.4 (PI) to 12.2 wt.% (PI-PETP 1 wt.%). Composites with PETP content between 1-10 wt.% have almost the same fraction of non-cyclized amide group, around 12 wt.% The increase of PETP content more than 10 wt.% gives rise to low amount of amide group, about 10.2 wt., which is close to amide content in the initial PI. It can be speculated that PETP content above 10 wt.% evokes deep separation of two polymers.
