56: # SYNTHESIS OF MDI-6FDA POLYIMIDE POWDER
57: 
58: The polymerization process was done in a glovebox under a high-purity nitrogen environment. A mixture of  $10\mathrm{mmol}$  MDI and  $10\mathrm{mmol}$  of 6FDA was reacted in NMP at ambient temperature for  $15\mathrm{min}$ . After  $15\mathrm{min}$  of stirring, the solution mixture was heated at  $100^{\circ}\mathrm{C}$  for  $3.5\mathrm{h}$ . Then, the solution was left to cool and poured into a large amount of distilled water to obtain the precipitated PI powder. The resulting product was filtered, washed with methanol, and dried at  $100^{\circ}\mathrm{C}$  for  $5\mathrm{h}$  in a vacuum oven. The reaction is shown in Scheme 1. The MDI-6FDA PI (yield:  $95.5\%$ ) structure was confirmed using IR and NMR analysis.
