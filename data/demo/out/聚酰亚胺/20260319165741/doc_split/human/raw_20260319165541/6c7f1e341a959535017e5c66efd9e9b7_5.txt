70: # $\mathrm{CO}_{2}$  Sorption
71: 
72: The equilibrium sorption isotherm of pure  $\mathrm{CO}_{2}$  in all films was volumetrically determined as a function of pressure at  $35^{\circ}\mathrm{C}$  by using a pressure-decay system. The sample was introduced into the chamber to degas the polymer film, and the sorption system was left overnight at  $35^{\circ}\mathrm{C}$ . Pure  $\mathrm{CO}_{2}$  was introduced into the sorption chamber. Penetrant uptake was recorded up to 50 atm as a function of time. All measurement data were obtained in the film state. A minimum of three samples were analyzed to confirm the reproducibility of the experimental results.
