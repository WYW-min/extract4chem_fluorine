61: # General procedure for the preparation of polyimide films (5-9)
62: 
63: To a solution of BAOB  $(0.495\mathrm{g},1.25\mathrm{mmol})$  in  $5\mathrm{ml}$  of DMAc, was added gradually bis-anhydride  $(1.25\mathrm{mmol})$ . The mixture was stirred at room temperature for  $2\mathrm{h}$  under argon. The obtained DMAc solution of poly(amic acid) was poured onto a glass plate, allowed to stand at room temperature overnight, and then heated from 60 to  $270^{\circ}\mathrm{C}$  (at a heating rate of  $1^{\circ}\mathrm{C / min}$ ) in a furnace. The film was then peeled off from the glass plate to obtain the PI film.
