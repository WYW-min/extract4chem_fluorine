192: # 4. Conclusions
193: 
194: In this study, PI films (PIa-c-t) were synthesized by the preparation of the HAT-T ternary anhydride monomer containing the hexaaazatriphenylene structure, the commercial dianhydride 6FDA and commercial diamines (SDA, TFDB and PABZ). The experimental results show that the PIs with the addition of hexaaazatriphenylene structure have high molecular weight and good mechanical properties. In addition, the introduction of the hexaaazatriphenylene structure has an important effect on the permeability and selectivity of a gas. Compared with the PI film without the hexaaazatriphenylene structure, the gas permeability of the PI film with the introduction of the hexaaazatriphenylene structure decreased, but the gas selectivity increased. The selectivity for  $\mathrm{CO}_{2} / \mathrm{CH}_{4}$  gas is 188.43, which surpasses the "2008 Robeson Upper Bound". This is because the introduction of the hexaaazatriphenylene structure can effectively reduce the interchain interaction and inhibit chain stacking. The rigid planar structure of hexaaazatriphenylene can also improve the selectivity for  $\mathrm{CO}_{2}$  gas. Therefore, this study is of great significance for the design and synthesis of cross-linked PIs and their application in gas separation membranes.
